üåü What is Spaced Repetition?

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that incorporates increasing intervals of time between subsequent review of previously learned material to exploit the psychological spacing effect.

üìö Benefits of Anki

Anki uses an algorithm to schedule reviews at optimal intervals, helping you remember information more efficiently. It's particularly effective for language learning, medical studies, and exam preparation.
